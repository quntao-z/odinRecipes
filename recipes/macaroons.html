Macaroons

Description: Magnificent recipe for a French Dessert

Ingredients

  • 3 Egg Whites
  • ¼ Cup white sugar
  • 2 Cup confectioners sugar
  • 1 Cup almond flour
  • pinch of salt
  • ¼ tsp cream of tartar

Raspberry Filling

  • ½ Cup salted butter
  • 1 Cup powdered sugar
  • 1 Cup 150 g fresh raspberries

Lemon Filling

  • ½ Cup salted butter
  • 1 Cup powdered sugar
  • Lemon Curd
  • Cream/Milk
